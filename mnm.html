About Us

Marcia & Marc, a live Singing Duo based in Chennai, India. They are known for their versatility, energy, and passion for music. With a shared love for performing, they bring life to every stage they step on, creating unforgettable experiences for their audiences.

Covering a wide range of musical genres, Marcia & Marc seamlessly blend styles such as pop, R&B, soul, jazz, acoustic, and classic hits. Their ability to adapt their sound allows them to connect with diverse audiences, whether it’s a relaxed acoustic evening or a high-energy celebration.

The duo has performed at both national and international events, showcasing their talent across different cultures and settings. From weddings and private functions to corporate events and large-scale performances, they consistently deliver professional and engaging shows.

With their unique vocal harmony, stage presence, and dedication to their craft, Marcia & Marc continue to captivate audiences and leave a lasting impression wherever they perform.
